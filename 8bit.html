THE 8BIT_PABLO VISUAL ALBUM

This is a small personal project I did after being inspired by the Kanye West album cover, THE LIFE OF PABLO

coincidently, a friend of mine gave me the nick name 8Bit_PABLO because I was doing 8bit art at the time.

As you can see, the background of each video shares similarities to Kanye's album cover

I then proceeded to mix it with some superhero movies I had seen, by choosing scenes I liked

and animated the characters quoting lines and playing out said scenes in my own way.
